Team Work

Become PART OF OUR TEAM

 At Ambulance Medic Limited we embrace the responsibility and challenges of providing a high-quality service. We put people first so that we can offer patients the care that they need and deserve. It is these values that ensure everyone at Ambulance Medic Limited  understands the important role they play in caring for our patients and that they receive the highest quality of care available. 

 Ambulance Medic Limited is a provider of emergency care in the Event Medical and Private Ambulance Sector across the UK.  We look for certain qualities in people who want to work with us:

  • Patient orientated
  • Caring
  • Team player
  • Punctual
  • Smart
  • Able to use their own initiative

We like to think that working with our team is not just about the money, it’s about enjoying working with us and ensuring that our service users receive the best possible care.  As a provider of care we constantly strive to improve the service we provide and we look for this quality in all of our team members.

Following an increase in demand we are now looking to increase our part-time team members who are caring, professional, punctual and smart. If you are looking to work with a professional, caring medical provider then look no further.  We are looking for event and ambulance staff to provide medical support across the UK and Europe.
