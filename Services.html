Services we offer

Event medical cover

 We have a highly trained team of medical and rescue personnel available to fulfil your statutory Health and Safety requirements for all types of event, whether small community event, large scale extreme sports event, construction project, music festival or TV and film  

 We will work closely with you to develop your medical plan that calculates your exact needs and to ensure you meet all legal requirements, please do not hesitate to contact us for further information 

Workforce Provider 

Our staff include registered doctors, HCPC Paramedics, NMC Nurses, HPA IHCD Ambulance Technicians,  Radiographers, SIA Security Staff, Chiropractors,  Physiotherapist & many more, What ever your staffing needs, we can help.


All our staff are DBS checked and throughly vetted saving you time and costly process of finding good reliable and trustworthy staff

tRAINING

Our experienced and highly trained instructors provide the highest quality courses possible. Our goal is to help individuals achieve their goals with ease . 

 We can provide appropriate Medical training to suit your business needs from schools to large businesses, outdoors and indoors.

​From a handful of delegates to a whole team we can tailor a course and venue to get your requirements.
